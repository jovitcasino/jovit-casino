About

Jovit Casiño

Hello, I'm Jovit Casiño, a graduate of the Technological Institute of the Philippines, holding a bachelor's degree in Information Technology. I proudly graduated as a Cum Laude, a testament to my dedication to academic excellence.

During my time at the institution, I had the privilege of serving as the Secretary of the IT Department Student Council for two consecutive academic years, from 2019 to 2020 and 2020 to 2021. This experience allowed me to actively contribute to the student community and foster leadership skills.

One of my greatest passions is the world of art. You can explore my artistic creations in the gallery available on my Facebook and Instagram profiles—don't hesitate to check them out! Additionally, I had the honor of previously serving as a Graphic Designer for the Sangguniang Kabataan in Brgy. Burgos, Rodriguez, Rizal, where I could channel my creative talents to benefit the community.

Thank you for taking the time to get to know me better!
